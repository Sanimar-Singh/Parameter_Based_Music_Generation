Problem

Generating quality music quickly presents challenges for beginners as well as experts. From mastering melodies and rhythms to perfecting harmonies, the process demands time and expertise. Bridging this gap between creativity and ease of use is a universal goal in music composition.

Objectives

The objectives of our project are twofold: to democratize music creation and to leverage the capabilities of Artificial Intelligence for personalized music generation. We aim to develop a user-friendly system that allows individuals, regardless of their musical expertise, to craft unique music compositions tailored to their themes, emotions, or narratives.
